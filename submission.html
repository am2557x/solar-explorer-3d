// 04 · Deeper Understanding Statement

OrbitControls with damping — full drag-to-rotate and scroll-to-zoom beyond lab scope.
RGBELoader HDR environment map with ACES Filmic tonemapping for physically accurate image-based lighting.
Luminance-based colour variant system that traverses mesh children and updates THREE.Color while preserving dark detail regions.
